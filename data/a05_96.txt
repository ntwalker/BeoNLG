The Paris Psalter: Psalm 96
Verse Indeterminate Saxon



Rixað nu mid rihte      rice drihten; 
is eorðe nu      eac on blisse, 
and þæs fægerne      gefean habbað 
ealanda mænig      ut on garsæcge.
