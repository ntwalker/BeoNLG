Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 121
Verse Indeterminate Saxon



Fiat pax in uirtute tua, et abundantia in turribus tuis.
Sy ðe on ðinum mægne sib           mæst and fyrmest,  
and on þinum torrum wese           tidum genihtsum.
